My Bio

I am a student at Nashville Software School learning how to problem solve in coding so that I might become a junior developer someday! I enjoy video gaming with friends, reading, writing and idea brainstorming.

My work with Michael Connors

05/17/15-present

Recently I've been working with Michael Connors, a local musician, to write songs and children's books. Michael started a scholarship charity called Kids that Rock which focused on providing a way for high school kids to earn a scholarship through music with community support. I assisted the launch show and we were able to provide a $500 dollar scholarship!
